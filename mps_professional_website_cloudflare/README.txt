Upload instructions for Cloudflare Pages:
1. Unzip this folder.
2. Go to Cloudflare > Workers & Pages > your Pages project.
3. Choose Direct Upload.
4. Upload index.html and style.css, or upload this ZIP if Cloudflare accepts ZIP upload.
5. Deploy.
